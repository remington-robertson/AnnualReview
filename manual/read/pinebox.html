pinebox





killing the death industry




1

Concept

An online resource for comparing local funeral home services, purchasing caskets and managing family accommodations.


Users streamline the pain of managing the loss of a loved one by choosing from funeral services that meet their price point, date(s), flexibility, level of service and reputation. They can buy a casket of their choice from wholesale suppliers without extreme markups or persuasion from salespeople.

3

Market

U.S. Funeral Market: $20.7B

2.4M Funerals in the US per year

57M Americans who are 60 years or older

76M Americans will be 60 years or older in 2020

Typically a funeral costs between $8,000-$10,000 with the casket at $2,300 and Funeral Director's Fees at $1,500

Caskets selling at $1295 at a funeral home are sold for $325 at wholesale

41% of Americans chose to cremate in 2012; 1/3 of those said cost was a primary factor

14% of funeral homes are owned by publicly traded companies

The largest, Service Corporation International, grossed $2.4B in 2012

They sit on $7.4B in future revenue from pre-need funeral sales

4

Competition

The battle is against old world funeral home conglomerates and related incumbents. No startups are threatening the space.


(It should be noted that caskets can be purchased from regional distribution companies, Walmart and Amazon with low markup. These players generally lack diversity within their product lines, respectfully, due to geographical constraints from warehousing and shipping).

5

Evolution

Phase 1:

Casket sales, funeral service comparison, family (accommodations) management.

Phase 2

Suite of tools to help execute wills, navigate life insurance policies and inheritance issues.

Phase 3

End-to-end "pre-need" solutions (wills, trusts, policies) for everyone.

6

Revenue

Caskets

Last year Texas had 167,000 deaths on record. Our target goal for the first year is capturing 1-2% of this market with an estimated $100 net profit from each casket sale. This puts our first annual gross revenue at $167-334,000.

Pre-Need

Since the money will be held in escrow, we gain interest on the accounts over time until they are used for the funeral proceedings. These numbers are impossible to predict, but with the success of our platform, pre-need will bring in large amounts of revenue that are predictable and reliant.
